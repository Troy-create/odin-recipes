NSHIMA RECIPE

zambia is the country very well known for this meal, it is so loved that it is even thier everyday food.

imagine a meal that you can eat like everyday. "well thats how much it is loved."

INGREDIENTS

doesn't require alot i guess thats why its affordable everyday.

  • maize meal
  • chicken/beef

HOW TO COOK

  1. Pour about 3 Â½ pints of water into a pot and bring it almost to a boil.
  2. Throw in four handfuls of maize meal.
  3. Stir vigorously with a wooden spoon, until the maize meal thoroughly mixes with the water.
  4. Let it thicken and wait for the mixture to start bubbling
  5. Cook for about 10 minutes on medium heat.
  6. Then add another four handfuls of maize meal while continuously stirring.
  7. When the mixture reaches its desired thickness, cover the pot and switch off the stove.
  8. After about two minutes, scoop it out and serve with beef or chicken.
